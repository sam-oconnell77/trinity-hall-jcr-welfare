You can make a free GP appointment by ringing the College Health Services on +353 1 896 1591. Free drop-in appointments are also available Monday to Friday at 9:30am and 2pm — these are often busy, so aim to queue at least 30 minutes early. All appointments take place in the College Health Services.

Contact TCD Counselling Services on 01 896 1497. Counselling is free, confidential, and non-judgemental for all Trinity students — have your student number ready when you call. Drop-in appointments run at 1pm and 1:30pm term time at the Counselling Services on Nassau Street. Weekly counselling appointments are also available in Halls — ask about availability when booking.

The College Health Services offer two clinics every Wednesday (9:30–11:30am and 1:30–3:30pm):

  • Mini Screen with a Nurse — for those without symptoms; tests for HIV, Hepatitis B, Syphilis, Chlamydia, and Gonorrhoea.
  • Sexual Health Clinic with Doctor & Nurse — more detailed; suitable for those with symptoms, men who have sex with men, or those at higher risk.

Book via the Health Centre reception. A €20 deposit is required (€5 refunded when you attend; 48hrs notice needed to cancel).

Other Dublin options:

  • Gay Men's Health Service, Baggot Street Hospital — free, walk-in Tuesday & Wednesday evenings from 4:30pm. gmhs.ie
  • GUIDE Clinic, St James's Hospital — free, walk-in Monday from 9am & Tuesday/Thursday from 1:30pm. guideclinic.ie
